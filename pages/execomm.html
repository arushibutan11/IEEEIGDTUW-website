EXECOMM

The execomm is the functional unit of IEEE IGDTUW that undertakes and monitors various activities of the branch.


The roles carried out are:
  • IEEE vice chairperson internal - SIGs, weekly IEEE events, any activity within college.
  • IEEE vice chairperson external - coordinating workshops, seminars, deciding who will go for meets, etc.
  • IEEE general secretary - basic record keeping work, to call all meetings, keep records of each event/activity organised.
  • WIE vice chairperson - organise self defence workshops, seminars, weekly activities on social issues, ngo work etc.
  • WIE general secretary - to assist vice chairperson in anything record and basic record keeping work.
  • PRO - facebook page, facebook group, keep it constantly updated, to post activity details, publicity, etc.
  • Website Administrator - to update the IGDTUW IEEE website whenever required.
  • Publication head - to overlook the editorial and creative designing of the e- newsletter of IGDTUW IEEE.

IEEE Chairperson: The IEEE Chairperson looks at the overall functioning of the student branch

WIE Chairperson: Guides and Inpires the girls to work in the direction of promoting engineering.

IEEE Treasurer: Manages the financial aspect of IEEE IGDTUW.

IEEE Technical Head: Monitors the technical progress of students in the campus.
