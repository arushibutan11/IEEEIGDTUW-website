WIE

We are very pleased to announce the formation of the IEEE IGDTUW WIE affinity group. IEEE's Women in Engineering Group is a community of women and men supporting women and girls in their endeavors. The group is dedicated to promoting the importance of women in engineering, increasing career opportunities and providing a professional, social and support network for women.


  • Our Mission

    WIE's mission is to Inspire, Engage, Encourage and Empower women worldwide. It is dedicated to facilitating the development of programs and activities that promote the entry into and the retention of women in the Engineering and science related programs and enhance the career advancement of women in the profession.

Our Vision

IEEE IGDTUW WIE is engaged in building a collaborative work environment to:

  • Support IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) activities worldwide.
  • Prove outreach to young women interested in science and engineering.
  • Prove outreach to young women interested in science and engineering.
  • Facilitate the exchange and debate of issues associated with women in engineering.
  • Women are one growing source for the enterprises. They offer complementarity in projects and in the decision making process.

WIE's dedication
  • RECOGNIZES women's outstanding achievements in electrical and electronics engineering through IEEE Awards.
  • ORGANIZES receptions at major technical conferences to enhance networking and to promote membership in WIE.
  • ADVOCATES women in leadership roles in IEEE governance and career advancement for women in the profession.
  • PROVIDES assistance with the formation of new WIE Affinity Groups and supports ongoing activities.
  • ADMINISTERS the IEEE Student-Teacher and Research Engineer/Scientist (STAR) Program to mentor young women in junior high school and high school.
  • PROMOTES member grade advancement for women to the grades of Senior Member and Fellow.
  • FACILITATES the development of programs and activities that promote the entry into and retention of women in engineering programs.

SOME OF OUR FUTURE ACTIVITIES INCLUDE:
  • SPARSH, The outreach program: A social responsibility program in collaboration with KILKARI FOUNDATION for providing girls at kilkari home free education and touch their lives in every possible manner.
  • A self defence workshop to equipe the young girls with the power to protect themselves and become self reliant
  • Talk sessions with famous personalities.
